About Us

Learn about our journey, experience, and commitment to excellence in medical equipment supply.

PT Gravia Citra Lestari

PT Gravia Citra Lestari was established on November 11, 2017, as a holding company specializing in trading and supplying medical equipment with a primary focus on Blood Transfusion Instruments & Products.

With over 20 years of industry experience dating back to 2000, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for hospitals, clinics, blood donation units, and medical institutions across Indonesia.

As an authorized distributor from domestic manufacturers and PT Panatha Citra Lestari, we offer a comprehensive range of both imported and locally manufactured medical products, ensuring quality and reliability in every solution we provide.

Led by experienced professionals in the medical equipment industry, we are committed to distributing medical innovation and leading the way in healthcare excellence.
